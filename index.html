About Andaman

A tropical paradise in the Bay of Bengal

The Andaman Islands are a breathtaking archipelago of over 570 islands, with pristine beaches, lush rainforests, and some of the world's most vibrant coral reefs. Whether you're an adventure seeker or simply looking to unwind, Andaman offers an experience unlike any other destination in India.

Our Services

Everything you need for a perfect island getaway

🤿 Scuba Diving

Explore vibrant coral gardens and exotic marine life with our certified PADI instructors. Suitable for beginners and pros alike.

🏝️ Island Tours

Guided day trips to Havelock, Neil Island, Ross Island, and more — by private speedboat or ferry.

🏕️ Beach Camping

Spend a night under the stars on a secluded beach with bonfire, dinner, and sunrise views included.

🐠 Snorkelling

Swim alongside sea turtles, clownfish, and rays at Elephant Beach and Radhanagar — no experience needed.

🚣 Kayaking

Glide through mangrove creeks and sea caves on guided kayak tours — peaceful, scenic, and easy.

💆 Spa & Wellness

Rejuvenate with beachside massage therapies and Ayurvedic treatments after a day of adventure.
